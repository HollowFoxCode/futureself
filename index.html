Month 4 of 12 — Honesty · Autumn
Dear Sarah,

I want to ask you something, and I need you not to skip past it the way you usually do when a question gets too close to the real thing.

That mug — the blue one with the chipped handle. You still reach for it first every morning, don't you? I know why. It isn't just habit. It's the closest you get, on an ordinary Tuesday, to feeling like you have permission to begin.

The version of you I am now knows what you're building. I won't tell you the ending — you'd spend the next six months trying to rush toward it. What I will tell you: the thing you're most afraid of isn't failure. It's being seen trying and still not making it. Notice that fear. Name it out loud at least once. Then decide whether it's worth handing your whole year to.

The coffee is getting cold. Start anyway.

With love from ten years forward,
You

Questions

What people ask first.

Is this AI-generated? How is it personal?
Yes — each letter is written by an AI given only your intake responses as context. It knows nothing about you except what you share, which is why the intake questions matter. The result reads like someone who has studied you carefully — because it has.
Do I write anything after signing up?
No. Complete the intake once (about 10 minutes), then receive letters for a year. Some subscribers reply to their letters — this adds richness to future ones, but it's entirely optional.
Is my information private?
Completely. Your intake is used only to write your letters. It is never shared, sold, or used for any other purpose. Request deletion at any time by emailing us.
How do I give this as a gift?
Select Annual at checkout and enter the recipient's name and email. They receive a welcome email with instructions for their own private intake. You won't see their letters — the subscription belongs entirely to them.
